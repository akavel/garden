🌱 seedlings — young, unrefined ideas that I’ve just planted—or old, unrefined ideas that need watering. If I am a diligent, caring gardener, they’ll grow into budding and maybe even ripe;
🌿 budding — maturing works that I have spent considerable time and energy cultivating but have not finished. They are teenagers who have outgrown their seedling status and may someday grow into ripe;
🌳 ripe — complete works that I have edited and published as a cohesive whole. They are similar to a traditional blog post published at a point in time in that way—though I still tend these over time;
🏝️ isles are unrestrained playgrounds. Transient pockets of spacetime. They are workshops and vantage points. They are wiki. They may appear and may disappear. You are welcome to them, but not guaranteed much, other than what's there here and now.
